About

An enthusiastic young choreographer, dancer and teacher who always have a passion to contribute to the field of Indian classical dance forms; Bharatanatyam and Mohiniyattam, in which he has been taking systematic training since last one and half decades.

Being a student he has always given his best in the tests and possessed both bachelor and master degree in Bharatanatyam with high marks. He enjoys a divine bliss while giving training to the aspiring students and feels it is his responsibility to hand over the great dance forms to the next generation.

He can prepare students for a professional career in a dance related field and is able to teach both adults and children alike. He has recognised qualification in Dance Teaching from accredited bodies and has been working as a freelance dance teacher for more than 10 years.
